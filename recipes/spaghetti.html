Spaghetti

This is a simple and delicious weeknight spaghetti recipe using meat sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound dried spaghetti noodles
  • 1 jar of spaghetti sauce
  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 cup of water

Steps to Prepare

  1. Bring water to boil in pot
  2. Add spaghetti and boil for 11 minutes
  3. While spaghetti is cooking, put sauce into seperate pot and heat slowly
  4. When spaghetti is finished cooking, strain and put back into pot
  5. Mix sauce and spaghetti
